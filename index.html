Non Sibi, not for oneself alone

What the village gives back

  • The Scarsdale Foundation, nearly a century old, has given roughly $1 million in need based college scholarships over the past decade, including $229,000 to 48 students for 2025 to 2026.
  • The Scarsdale Volunteer Ambulance Corps has been all volunteer for more than 50 years, usually on scene within four minutes. In 2022 alone it administered close to 20,000 COVID tests and vaccines, including free shots for homebound residents.
  • The Scarsdale Adult School, founded in 1938, fills close to 10,000 seats a year and is open to anyone regardless of where they live, with tuition kept low so all can attend.
  • The Scarsdale Bowl, awarded every year since 1943, honors a resident who has given unselfishly of time and effort to the civic welfare of the community.

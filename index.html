    About this almanac & how to search

    What is The Dale Ledger?

    The Dale Ledger is a free, unofficial Scarsdale property almanac. It compiles public assessment rolls for roughly 5,500 residential parcels across the 2026 tentative roll, 2025 final roll, and 2024 tentative roll (SWIS 555000, Westchester County, New York). Search by street address, owner name, parcel ID, elementary school zone, full market value, or estimated property tax.

    What you can look up

    • Property values — assessed value, land value, and full market value on the roll
    • Estimated property tax — county, school, and village components from published mill rates (not an official bill)
    • School zones — Edgewood, Fox Meadow, Greenacres, Heathcote, and Quaker Ridge elementary zones
    • Building permits — pools, renovations, and filings from village PROS records
    • Board news — Planning Board, BAR, ZBA, and Historic Preservation agendas
    • Honor Roll — editorial superlatives (highest value, biggest tax, largest lot, and more)

    Example searches

    Try plain English: heathcote over 3m, tax over 80k, palmer ave, quaker ridge, under 1m, biggest tax, or an owner surname. Deep links work: ?year=2026&q=heathcote&parcel=13.01.15.
